Each row is one bone or sprite's own timeline. Its diamonds are that part's own keyframes — a pose at one instant. Between two consecutive keys on the same row, the part blends from the left key's pose to the right key's pose; rows are independent, so different parts key at different times.

Marker shape = how it blends into the next key

Linear — steady blend
Eased — quadratic/cubic curve
Hold — snaps, no blend until the next key
Sprite swap — the image changes here, not just its position

Row color & state

Pink = bone · Blue = sprite
Dimmed = exists in the rig, but not part of the pose at the playhead
